Section 6 QCL Impact Report

We need to do an Impact Report annual(Spring) and semi-annual(Summer)

6.1 Zoom - Reports

  • Go on to Zoom and Export the same reports you have before for check in to put in the archive and double check the check ins
    • Zoom > Reports > Usage > change the dates to the workshop > export participants with unique users with meeting data
    • Zoom > Surveys > Export Survey
  • Put them into a BOX folder and share the folder with Program Coordinator

6.2 Localist – Reports

Before you download, make sure you have checked in everyone who came to the workshop.

Go to workshop

Click on View Confirmed Tickets

Click on Export CSV

A email will be sent to your email account

Click on the link

It will download the excel file or CSV You will receive one at a time
Put all the files in a BOX folder and share it will the Program Coordinator
